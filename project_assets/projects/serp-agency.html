SERP Agency

SERP Agency was a digital marketing agency dedicated to helping businesses thrive online. Founded in April 2019 as SERPCO, LLC, it quickly grew under the leadership of co-founder Devin Schumacher.

Achievements

  • Achieved annual revenues between $2-$3.5 million
  • Bootstrap funded from zero to over 25 employees
  • Successfully assisted more than 1300 businesses across 217 industries
  • Generated over a quarter-billion dollars in new revenue for clients
  • Sold in a multi 7-figure acquisition in 2024
